About Chalisas and Stotram

"Chalisa" is a Hindu devotional poem or prayer consisting of 40 verses, typically praising a specific deity. "Stotram" is a similar devotional hymn, often with a shorter verse count, both used to extol the virtues and powers of a particular god in Hinduism.

The Hanuman Chalisa

The Hanuman Chalisa praises the power and other qualities of Hanuman including his strength, courage, wisdom, celibacy (brahmacharya), and devotion to Rama.

The Gayatri Chalisa

Gayatri Chalisa is a Hindu devotional hymn consisting of 40 verses, dedicated to Goddess Gayatri, the embodiment of the Vedic 'Gayatri Mantra,' which is considered a prayer to the divine solar energy.

The Navgrah Chalisa

Navagraha Chalisa is a 40 verse prayer to the 9 planets. Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu and Ketu, the group of these nine planets, is known as Navagraha. Get Book

The Santoshi Maa Chalisa

The Santoshi Chalisa encapsulates the boundless love and grace of Maa Santoshi, who, like a caring mother, nurtures her children and protects them from harm.

The Saraswati Chalisa

The Saraswati Chalisa is a Hindu prayer and hymn to the goddess Saraswati. It is a collection of 40 verses that praise the goddess and ask for her blessings. The prayer is often recited to seek wisdom, knowledge, creativity, and academic success.

The Shiv Chalisa

A "Shiva Chalisa" is a Hindu devotional hymn consisting of 40 verses dedicated to Lord Shiva, one of the primary deities in Hinduism, praising his divine qualities and seeking his blessings to overcome obstacles and hardships in life.

Get Book
The Shiv Tandav Stotram

Shiv Tandav Stotram was written by Ravana, the asura King and devotee of Shiva. Shiv Tandava Stotram is a hymn written and sung by Ravana in praise of Lord Shiva. The Shiv Tandav Stotram represents the cosmic dance of Lord Shiva that eliminates all negative energies. The hymn includes 15 verses and each verse describes the fearless Shiva and his eternal beauty in great detail.

The Laxmi Chalisa

The Lakshmi Chalisa is full of verses that praise the Goddess of wealth & invite prosperity, health, beauty & success in our careers.

The SundarKand

Sundara Kanda is about Hanuman's adventures in his search for Sita, who was abducted by Ravana to Lanka. The book emphasizes Hanuman's strength, selflessness, and devotion to Rama.
