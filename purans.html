About 18 MahaPurans

The 18 Mahapuranas are a collection of Hindu religious texts that are considered dogma by Hindus. The Mahapuranas cover a wide range of topics, including mythology, astronomy, genealogy, religious sites, and geographic information. The 18 Mahapuranas are: Brahma, Padma, Vishnu, Shiva, Bhagavata, Narada, Markandeya, Agni, Bhavisya, Brahmavaivarta, Linga, Varaha, Skanda, Vamana, Kurma, Matsya, Garuda, and Brahmanda.
