The Vedas

About Vedas

The Vedas are a collection of religious poetry, ritual formulas, and explanatory prose that are considered to be the oldest holy books in Hinduism. The word "Veda" means "knowledge" in Sanskrit.

Books

Rigveda

The Rigveda is the oldest known Vedic Sanskrit text. The sounds and texts of the Rigveda have been orally transmitted since the 2nd millennium BCE.

Get Book
YajurVeda

The Yajurveda is the Veda primarily of prose mantras for worship rituals. An ancient Vedic Sanskrit text, it is a compilation of ritual-offering formulas that were said by a priest while an individual performed ritual actions such as those before the yajna fire.

Get Book
SamaVeda

The Samaveda is the Veda of melodies and chants. It is an ancient Vedic Sanskrit text, and is one of the sacred scriptures in Hinduism. One of the four Vedas, it is a liturgical text which consists of 1,875 verses. All but 75 verses have been taken from the Rigveda.

Get Book
AtharvaVeda

The Atharvaveda is the "knowledge storehouse of atharvāṇas, the procedures for everyday life". The text is the fourth Veda, and is a late addition to the Vedic scriptures of Hinduism.
